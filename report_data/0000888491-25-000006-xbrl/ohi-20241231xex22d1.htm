Subsidiary guarantors of guaranteed securities

​

As of December 31, 2024, the Partnership has fully and unconditionally guaranteed the outstanding senior notes of the Company identified below:

●5.250% Senior Notes due 2026
●4.50% Senior Notes due 2027
●4.750% Senior Notes due 2028
●3.625% Senior Notes due 2029
●3.375% Senior Notes due 2031
